Khan el-Khalili

Khan el-Khalili is a famous historic market and bazaar located in the heart of Islamic Cairo, Egypt. It is one of the oldest markets in the Middle East, with a history dating back to the 14th century

Khan el-Khalili is renowned for its vibrant atmosphere, traditional architecture, and a wide array of shops and stalls offering a variety of goods. Visitors can explore the narrow winding streets and find shops selling items such as spices, textiles, jewelry, traditional crafts, antiques, souvenirs, and much more.

Khan el-Khalili is also well-known for its historic buildings and landmarks. One of the prominent landmarks in the area is the Al-Hussein Mosque, an important religious site and a popular gathering place for locals.
